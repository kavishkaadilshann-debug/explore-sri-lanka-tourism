Travel Tips & Guide

Essential information for your journey

✈️ Best Time to Visit

December to April is perfect for the west/south beaches and hill country. From May to September, head north and east for optimal sunshine.

🚕 Local Transport

Use local trains for epic scenic routes (like Kandy to Ella) and pick budget-friendly Tuk-Tuks (PickMe/Uber apps work great in cities).

🍛 Food Guide

Don't miss out on traditional Rice & Spicy Curry, Egg Hoppers, Kottu Roti, and drinking fresh King Coconut (Thambili) daily.

🛡️ Safety Tips

Stay properly hydrated, dress modestly when entering sacred temples (cover shoulders and knees), and secure your valuables.
