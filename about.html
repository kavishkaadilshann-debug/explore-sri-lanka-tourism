About Sri Lanka

The Pearl of the Indian Ocean

🇱🇰 Introduction

Sri Lanka is a beautiful island nation located in the Indian Ocean. It is famous for its breathtaking landscapes, rich cultural heritage, ancient cities, and warm hospitality.

🌴 Natural Beauty

From golden beaches and lush tea plantations to waterfalls and misty mountains, Sri Lanka offers diverse natural attractions for every traveler.

🏛️ Cultural Heritage

With over 2,500 years of history, Sri Lanka is home to ancient kingdoms, sacred temples, and UNESCO World Heritage Sites.

🐘 Wildlife & Nature

Sri Lanka is known for its wildlife, including elephants, leopards, whales, and hundreds of bird species found in national parks.

🍛 Traditional Cuisine

Sri Lankan cuisine is rich in flavor, featuring rice and curry, seafood dishes, tropical fruits, and unique spices.

🎉 Festivals & Traditions

The country celebrates colorful festivals such as Sinhala and Tamil New Year, Vesak, and the famous Kandy Esala Perahera.

📌 Quick Facts

  • Capital: Sri Jayawardenepura Kotte
  • Commercial Capital: Colombo
  • Population: Over 22 Million
  • Official Languages: Sinhala & Tamil
  • Currency: Sri Lankan Rupee (LKR)

✈️ Why Visit Sri Lanka?

  • Scenic Hill Country
  • Wildlife Safaris
