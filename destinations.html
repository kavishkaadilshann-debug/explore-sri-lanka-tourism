Top Destinations

Places you must visit in your lifetime

Sigiriya

Ancient rock fortress and UNESCO site.

Sigiriya is an ancient rock fortress in Sri Lanka and a UNESCO World Heritage Site. It is famous for its large rock, beautiful frescoes, and ancient palace ruins built by King Kashyapa.

Ella

Beautiful mountains and train views.

Ella is a town in the central hills of Sri Lanka, known for its stunning mountain views, tea plantations, and the famous Nine Arch Bridge. It is popular for trekking.

Galle

Historic Dutch colonial fort.

Galle is known for its historic Dutch colonial fort, a UNESCO World Heritage Site. The fort area is filled with charming streets, boutique shops, and unique cafes.

Kandy

Beautiful mountain view & Culture

Kandy is known for its rich cultural heritage, vibrant markets, and the sacred Temple of the Tooth Relic, making it a hub of traditional Sri Lankan culture.

Anuradhapura

Ancient city with sacred temples.

Anuradhapura features well-preserved ruins of an ancient civilization. It is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and home to many massive, sacred Buddhist stupas.

Trincomalee

Beautiful beaches and clear waters.

Trincomalee is a coastal city known for its beautiful white-sand beaches, crystal clear whale-watching waters, and deep natural harbor history.

Jaffna

Rich Tamil culture and heritage.

Jaffna is a cultural gem in Northern Sri Lanka, famous for its majestic Kovils, historic fort, unique local lifestyle, and delicious northern cuisine.
